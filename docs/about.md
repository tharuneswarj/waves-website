# About Waves Company

*Last updated: March 2026*

---

## Who We Are

Waves Company is a design and manufacturing studio creating algorithmically crafted lighting objects and custom installations.

We were founded on curiosity — specifically, the curiosity of a designer who wanted to understand what 3D printing could actually do when pushed past its defaults.

The answer, it turned out, was quite a lot.

---

## How It Started

In mid-2024, a computational design practice led to a 3D printer. The intention was simple: close the gap between what was being modeled on screen and what could exist in a room.

The first real object wasn't a prototype or an experiment. It was a lamp, designed and printed as a gift for a mother who loved her home. An origami form. A CNC-milled wooden base. Wired by hand.

When it was switched on, something became clear. The way the printed layers diffused the light. The way the geometry created shadow gradients that no off-the-shelf shade could produce. The technology had something to say about light that hadn't been said yet.

That observation became Waves.

---

## The Name

The tilde. A small symbol on the keyboard — shaped like a wave. The same wave the printer traces across a surface. The same undulation that appears in the lamp forms. Light travels in waves. The name was already there.

---

## What We Do Differently

Most 3D printed lighting is made in vase mode. Simple extrusions. Forms chosen because they're easy to print.

We took a different approach. Custom toolpaths written in Grasshopper. Parametric logic that treats layer height, wall density, and extrusion direction as design variables. Structural thinking applied to objects that most people treat as purely decorative.

The result is lighting that behaves differently — in how it diffuses, how it throws shadow, how it sits in a room.

---

## Where We Are Now

Waves launched on the 1st of January 2026 with two designs — the Hourglass and the Ripple.

Since then, we have completed installations for offices and residences, and are currently working with architects and interior designers on custom lighting briefs.

The catalogue grows from real projects. Every piece we make for a specific space teaches us something that becomes the next object.

We are a small studio. We are building carefully.

## What We Believe

Objects deserve the same intellectual rigour as buildings.

Light is not decoration — it is atmosphere.

A well-considered lamp changes how a room feels in ways that are difficult to articulate and impossible to miss.

We design with computation.

We decide with judgment.
