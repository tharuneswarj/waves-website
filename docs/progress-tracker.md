## Key Decisions Made

- **Typography:** DM Serif Display + DM Sans + DM Mono (replacing Montserrat/Open Sans)
- **Positioning:** Design-led digital craft studio. Not a marketplace seller. Not a mass manufacturer.
- **B2B first:** Build portfolio through Koodu Architecture channel. D2C follows once brand infrastructure is in place.
- **Pricing:** ₹4,999–5,999 for D2C table lamps. Custom quoted for installations and B2B.
- **Catalogue strategy:** Grow from real projects. Every client brief becomes a new product.
- **Sustainability:** Deprioritised for now. Focus on brand clarity first.
- **Origin story:** A printer, a gift, and a tilde. The mom's lamp is the founding object.
